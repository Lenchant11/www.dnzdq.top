2. Services Description

Cyber Justice India provides the following services to victims of online fraud:

  • Case Evaluation: Free initial assessment of your fraud case
  • Legal Consultation: Professional advice on recovery options
  • Digital Forensics: Tracing and documenting digital evidence
  • Recovery Efforts: Coordinating with financial institutions and authorities
  • Legal Representation: Working with our network of legal professionals
  • Ongoing Support: Regular updates and guidance throughout the process

Important: We are not a law firm but work with registered advocates across India. Our services complement legal proceedings but do not replace formal legal representation where required.

7. Success Fee Model

Our success-based fee model aligns our interests with yours:

Fee Calculation

Success fees are calculated as follows:

  • Standard Cases: 20% of recovered amount
  • Complex Cases: 25% of recovered amount (involving multiple jurisdictions, sophisticated scams)
  • Simpler Cases: 15% of recovered amount (straightforward recovery with clear evidence)

What Constitutes "Recovery"

Recovery is defined as:

  • Funds successfully returned to your account
  • Bank transfers initiated to your account
  • Court-ordered restitution payments
  • Insurance claim payouts facilitated by our efforts

Note: The specific success fee percentage for your case will be clearly defined in your service agreement based on case complexity and estimated recovery difficulty.
