FRAUD RECOVERY SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made and entered into as of [Date]

1. PARTIES

Service Provider: Cyber Justice India (hereinafter referred to as "Provider")

Address: [Provider's Address]

Email: legal@cyberjusticeindia.in

Client: [Client Full Name] (hereinafter referred to as "Client")

Address: [Client Address]

Email: [Client Email]

Phone: [Client Phone]

2. SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED

Provider agrees to provide the following services to Client:

  • Comprehensive evaluation of the fraud case
  • Digital forensics and evidence collection
  • Coordination with financial institutions and relevant authorities
  • Legal consultation and strategy development
  • Recovery efforts including communication with involved parties
  • Regular updates on case progress

Case Details: This agreement pertains to the fraud case involving an estimated amount of [Amount] lost through [Type of Fraud].

3. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Client agrees to:

  • Provide complete and accurate information regarding the fraud incident
  • Submit all relevant documentation and evidence promptly
  • Cooperate with Provider and provide necessary authorizations
  • Make timely decisions regarding case strategy when requested
  • Notify Provider of any changes in contact information
  • Pay fees as outlined in Section 4 of this agreement

4. FEE STRUCTURE AND PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 Retainer Fee: Client shall pay a non-refundable retainer fee of ₹5,000 upon execution of this agreement to initiate services.

4.2 Success Fee: Provider shall receive a success fee of [15-25%] of the gross amount recovered on behalf of Client.

4.3 No Recovery, No Fee Guarantee: If no funds are recovered, Client will not owe any success fees. The retainer fee remains non-refundable as compensation for initial work performed.

4.4 Payment Terms: Success fees are due within 7 days of Client's receipt of recovered funds. Late payments may incur interest charges of 1.5% per month.

5. TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 Term: This agreement shall commence on the effective date and continue until the earlier of: (a) successful recovery of funds; (b) mutual agreement to terminate; or (c) 12 months from the effective date.

5.2 Termination by Client: Client may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the termination date.

5.3 Termination by Provider: Provider may terminate this agreement if Client fails to fulfill responsibilities, provides false information, or fails to make required payments.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information shared during the course of this engagement. Provider will not disclose Client's identity or case details without explicit permission, except as required by law or necessary for recovery efforts.

7. DISCLAIMER AND LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

7.1 No Guarantee of Results: Provider makes no guarantees, warranties, or representations regarding the outcome of recovery efforts. While Provider will employ best efforts, success depends on numerous factors beyond Provider's control.

7.2 Limitation of Liability: Provider's total liability under this agreement, regardless of the form of action, shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client to Provider under this agreement.

7.3 No Consequential Damages: In no event shall Provider be liable for any indirect, special, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages, including but not limited to lost profits, emotional distress, or business interruption.

7.4 Third-Party Actions: Provider shall not be liable for actions or inactions of third parties, including financial institutions, law enforcement, or legal professionals involved in the recovery process.

7.5 Client Verification: Client acknowledges that Provider relies on information provided by Client and shall not be liable for inaccuracies in such information.

7.6 Force Majeure: Provider shall not be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from causes beyond its reasonable control.

8. INDEMNIFICATION

Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Provider, its employees, and affiliates from any claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from: (a) Client's breach of this agreement; (b) inaccurate information provided by Client; or (c) Client's failure to comply with applicable laws.

9. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

9.1 Governing Law: This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

9.2 Dispute Resolution: Any disputes arising under this agreement shall first be attempted to be resolved through good faith negotiation. If unresolved, parties agree to binding arbitration in New Delhi, India.

10. MISCELLANEOUS

10.1 Entire Agreement: This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, and agreements.

10.2 Amendment: This agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.

10.3 Severability: If any provision of this agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

10.4 Notices: All notices required under this agreement shall be in writing and sent to the addresses specified in Section 1.

ACCEPTANCE AND SIGNATURES

The parties hereby acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this agreement.
