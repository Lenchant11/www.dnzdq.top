Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any fee to apply or join?
Absolutely not! We never charge application or registration fees. Our platform is completely free to join and use. We only make money when you do.
How quickly will I receive my first payment?
Most of our Irish members receive their first payment within 24 hours of completing their initial tasks. Payments are processed daily via WhatsApp after tasks pass our quality checks.
What equipment do I need to get started?
A smartphone and stable internet connection are sufficient for most tasks. Some roles may prefer a computer, but it's not necessary for the majority of our opportunities.
How will you contact me with tasks?
We communicate primarily through WhatsApp. After you apply, you'll receive step-by-step instructions via WhatsApp. All tasks, support, and payments are managed through this platform for convenience and security.
Do I need previous experience?
No previous experience is required for most positions. We provide clear instructions and support to help you succeed. Basic digital literacy is helpful but we'll guide you through everything.
